## Partner Integration Roadmap for Ideation Validator

### Target Integrations

| Partner/SaaS/API                | Free-Tier Limits                     | Integration Effort | Value-Add (User Job Solved)                                         | Affiliate/Revenue-Share |
|----------------------------------|--------------------------------------|--------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------|
| **Zapier**                       | 5 Zaps, 100 Tasks/month              | M                  | Automate workflows by connecting ideation outputs to other apps.    | Yes                      |
| **Airtable**                     | 1,200 records per base               | M                  | Organize and manage validated ideas in a user-friendly database.     | Yes                      |
| **Notion**                       | 1,000 blocks per workspace           | S                  | Create a collaborative space for ideation and feedback collection.   | Yes                      |
| **Trello**                       | 10 boards, 250 cards per board       | S                  | Visualize and track the ideation process with Kanban-style boards.    | Yes                      |
| **Figma**                        | 3 projects, 2 editors                 | M                  | Design and prototype ideas visually, enhancing user experience.       | Yes                      |
| **Slack**                        | 10 integrations, 5GB storage         | S                  | Facilitate team communication and feedback on ideas in real-time.     | Yes                      |
| **Google Sheets**                | 15GB total storage                    | S                  | Analyze and validate ideas using familiar spreadsheet functionalities. | No                       |
| **Typeform**                     | 100 responses/month                   | S                  | Collect user feedback on ideas through interactive forms.             | Yes                      |

### Rationale for Selection

1. **Zapier**: Enables users to automate their workflows by connecting ideation outputs to various applications, enhancing productivity and efficiency. It has a revenue-share model that can drive additional income.

2. **Airtable**: Provides a flexible database solution for users to organize and manage their validated ideas, making it easier to track progress and collaborate. The affiliate program offers potential revenue opportunities.

3. **Notion**: Offers a collaborative workspace where users can gather feedback and iterate on their ideas. This integration enhances the ideation process by fostering teamwork and creativity.

4. **Trello**: Allows users to visualize their ideation process through boards, making it easier to manage tasks and ideas. This integration provides a structured approach to tracking progress.

5. **Figma**: Enables users to create visual representations of their ideas, which is crucial for prototyping and user testing. This integration enhances the overall user experience and engagement.

6. **Slack**: Facilitates real-time communication and feedback among team members, streamlining the ideation process. This integration is vital for collaboration and quick decision-making.

7. **Google Sheets**: While it does not have a revenue-share model, it is widely used for data analysis and validation, making it a valuable tool for users to analyze their ideas quantitatively.

8. **Typeform**: Engages users in collecting feedback through interactive forms, which can improve the validation process of ideas. The affiliate program can provide additional revenue streams.

### Conclusion

Integrating with these SaaS/APIs will enhance the functionality of the Ideation Validator, providing significant value to indie hackers and creators while also opening up potential revenue opportunities through affiliate programs. Prioritizing integrations with revenue-share models will maximize financial benefits for Axentx.
